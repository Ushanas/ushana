DONATIONS TOURISTS COULD MAKE FOR THE BETTERMENT OF THE TURTLE POPULATION IN THE COUNTRY

There are several initiatives that tourists can donate to for the betterment of the turtle population in Sri Lanka.

One of the most popular options is to donate to local sea turtle conservation organizations, such as the Turtle Conservation Project or the Kosgoda Sea Turtle Conservation Project. These organizations work to protect turtle nests, rehabilitate injured turtles, and educate the public about the importance of conservation efforts.

Another great way to support the turtle population in Sri Lanka is to donate to beach clean-up efforts. Litter on the beach can disrupt turtle nesting sites and endanger the hatchlings, so keeping the beaches clean is crucial for their survival. You can donate to organizations like the Sri Lanka Wildlife Conservation Society, which coordinates beach clean-up events and promotes sustainable tourism.
